About Us

WE BELIEVE EVERY STORY STARTS WITH A SMILE

Dental Stories is a Dental Clinic owned by Dr. Jenish Parmar and Dr. Poonam Sarda, both specializing in Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics. We do our small part of taking care of your oral health so that you can share your stories with a bright smile.

We are a team of dedicated dental professionals committed to providing exceptional oral healthcare services to our patients. With 10+ years of experience and a passion for dentistry, we strive to create a hospitable environment with personalized care for your dental needs.
