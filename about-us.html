The Auther to Your Dental Stories

Dr. Poonam Sarda

She obtained her B.D.S. degree in 2016, followed by the successful completion of her M.D.S. degree in Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics, where she was honored with a silver medal from Maharashtra University of Health Sciences. Her expertise lies in minimally invasive treatment within the field of specialization. With a rich experience of 7 years in clinical dentistry, her primary focus is on delivering pain-free dental care. Dr. Sarda specializes in root canal treatment, anterior diastema closure using composites, and various tooth-colored restorations.

Dr. Jenish Parmar

He has a strong background in root canal therapy. Dr. Jenish Parmar has a BDS degree, an MDS in Endodontics, and an exceptional scholastic history. He also holds an MDS in Endodontics. Dr. Jenish Parmar specializes in endodontics, which is the study, diagnosis, and treatment of dental diseases affecting the tooth pulp and its surrounding tissues. He can carry out difficult root canal treatments with accuracy and efficiency because to his specialized training and expertise. Because of his love for the discipline of endodontics and commitment to his patients, Dr. Jenish Parmar is a respected expert in the area. He regularly provides top-notch treatment with accuracy and competence, whether it's detecting and treating dental diseases or carrying out challenging root canal surgeries.
