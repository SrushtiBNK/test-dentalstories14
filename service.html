Endodontics Services

Endodontic dentistry specializes in treating the interior of the tooth, focusing on root canals and pulp-related issues. This field aims to save damaged or infected teeth through precise procedures, preserving natural smiles and oral health.

  1. Root Canal Therapy
  2. Pulpotomy
  3. Apicoectomy (Surgical Root Canal)
  4. Root Canal Retreatment
  5. Treatment for Dental Abscesses

Conservative Dentistry Services

Conservative dentistry focuses on preserving and restoring teeth with minimal intervention. Dentists in this field prioritize preventive care, using techniques like dental fillings and sealants to treat cavities and protect teeth from further damage. By emphasizing early detection and conservative approaches, this discipline aims to maintain a patient's natural teeth for as long as possible.

  1. Dental Fillings
  2. Dental Crowns and Bridges
  3. Inlays and Onlays
  4. Tooth Restoration and Preservation
  5. Tooth-Colored Restorations
